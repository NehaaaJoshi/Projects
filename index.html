SCENE STRATEGIC COLLABORATIVE FOR ENGAGEMENT AND EVALUATION

EVALUATION AND ENGAGEMENT, OUR EXPERTISE

The Strategic Collaborative for Engagement and Evaluation (SCENE) is an innovative initiative that brings together experts in evaluation and/or engagement from all across the University of Florida to systematically collaborate with researchers from all disciplinary backgrounds in the preparation and submission of grant proposals. The experts from SCENE develop rigorous, feasible, and effective Evaluation and/or Outreach components that meet the evolving information and dissemination needs of the proposed projects. When the projects are funded, these experts implement their planned activities in close collaboration and communication with the projects’ teams.

Who we are

The Strategic Collaborative for Engagement and Evaluation (SCENE) is a hub of innovation that brings together faculty, staff, and students from multiple departments and colleges across the University of Florida to collaborate with other researchers in the preparation, submission, implementation, and evaluation of grants-funded proposals and projects as well as develop the capacity of UF faculty and organizations to advance the scholarship and practice of engagement and evaluation.

Mission

Establish an interconnected and active community of skilled faculty, staff, and students to improve engagement and evaluation in research, teaching, and extension initiatives with the purpose of maximizing their value and impact on the wider community.

Vision

Enhancing the institutional capacity of the University of Florida by transforming existing and new collaborations into valuable resources and opportunities to address social change and impact through engagement and evaluation. The concerted efforts of this interdisciplinary network will support a culture of project collaboration and promote the best practices in team science.

Goals
  • Create an enabling environment in which collaboration and creativity flourish
  • Increase success in securing funding for innovative projects developed by UF faculty
  • Improve human and organizational capacity for evaluation and engagement in team science
  • Enhance the University of Florida’s visibility and prestige in evaluation and engagement
  • Form an accessible and functional axis connecting reliable professionals and strengthening the educational and collaborative capabilities of UF scholars, practitioners, and students alike
